About me

Hello! I am Danushe Hlavenka, passionate about music and a teacher with years of experience in music teaching. My goal is to transmit the love and beauty of music to my students, helping them develop their talent and achieve their musical goals.

I offer personalized classes for students of all ages and levels, from beginners to advanced. My approach is based on a combination of traditional teaching techniques and innovative methods to ensure effective and fun learning.

In addition, I have extensive experience working with kids with special needs. I understand the unique learning styles and challenges they may face, and I adapt my teaching methods to create a supportive and inclusive environment for all students.

If you are interested in taking classes or have any questions, please feel free to contact me. I would be delighted to assist you in your musical journey.
